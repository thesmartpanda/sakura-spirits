Our Story

Born from a journey to Japan and a life-changing dram of Yamazaki 18 — Sakura Spirits exists to share the wonder of Japanese whisky with the world.

Rooted in Respect
for the Craft

It started with a single glass in a tiny Kyoto bar in 2018. The bartender — a woman who had spent thirty years studying whisky — poured a measure of Yamazaki 18 Year without a word and slid it across the counter.

That moment changed everything. We returned from Japan knowing that the world deserved better access to these extraordinary spirits. Sakura Spirits was founded the following year.

Today we work directly with distilleries, importers, and collectors to source expressions that are genuinely worth your time — and your money.

A Brief History

1923

Yamazaki Distillery Founded

Shinjiro Torii establishes Japan's first malt whisky distillery at the confluence of three rivers south of Kyoto.

1934

Masataka Taketsuru Opens Yoichi

Having trained in Scotland, Taketsuru founds Nikka in Hokkaido — choosing a climate reminiscent of his beloved Highlands.

2001

Japanese Whisky Goes Global

Nikka's single cask wins at the Whisky Magazine World Whiskies Awards, marking the moment the world sat up and took notice.

2018

The Glass in Kyoto

Our founder Emma Thornton has a life-changing encounter with Yamazaki 18 Year in a Kyoto bar. Sakura Spirits is conceived that evening.

2019

Sakura Spirits Founded

We open our doors — first as an online retailer, then expanding to a curated selection of the finest Japanese expressions available outside Japan.

2026

Seven Expressions, One Philosophy

Today we carry seven carefully chosen whiskies, each one a conversation with Japan's extraordinary distilling heritage.
